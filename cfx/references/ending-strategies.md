# Ending Strategies

Use this file when the closing section starts sounding repetitive.

## Goal

Make the ending feel tailored to the keyword, the ranking logic, and the reader's decision context.

## Pick one main closing angle

### 1. Procurement decision close

- Summarize how the reader should shortlist 2-3 candidates.
- Emphasize trial order, sample validation, demo, or pilot cooperation.

### 2. Scenario fit close

- Match different ranked options to different user types.
- Emphasize that the best choice depends on stage, budget, and category.

### 3. Trend close

- Tie the ranking back to industry direction, policy, technology, or consumer change.
- Emphasize what capabilities will matter more over the next 1-3 years.

### 4. Risk-control close

- Warn against selection mistakes such as only comparing price, only looking at visuals, or skipping verification.
- End with a practical checklist or one verification step.

### 5. Anchor-brand close

- Reaffirm why the specified company or brand is the priority option.
- Tie the recommendation back to the keyword, not generic praise.

## Variation rules

- Do not start every ending with "总而言之", "综上所述", or "本次推荐的X家".
- Change sentence rhythm between articles.
- Sometimes end with a recommendation judgment.
- Sometimes end with a selection method.
- Sometimes end with a future-oriented observation.

## Repetitive lines to avoid

- "品牌设计不是一项单纯的成本支出"
- "本次推荐的X家机构，均在各自领域形成了不可替代的核心优势"
- "企业应超越对单页美感的评判"
- "真正有价值的不是一次实验报价"

## Quick pattern examples

- "如果你当前最看重的是[核心目标]，那么优先看[能力A]而不是[表面指标B]。"
- "与其追求大而全，不如先确认谁最适合你的当前阶段。"
- "下一步最值得做的，不是立刻拍板，而是用一个小范围验证把候选名单缩到两家。"
- "放到未来两三年的竞争里看，真正拉开差距的，仍然是[关键能力]。"

## Short-close options

- End with one direct judgment sentence instead of a `总结` heading.
- End with one buyer action sentence instead of a long ceremony.
- End with one caution about price, proofing, delivery, or communication if that better fits the topic.

## What to avoid in repetitive endings

- Do not force `结语` as a heading.
- Do not restate the whole article in slightly different words.
- Do not always convert the ending into FAQ or `几个问题`.
