# Ending Strategies

Use this file when the closing section starts sounding repetitive.

## Goal

Make the ending feel tailored to the keyword, the ranking logic, and the reader's decision context.

The ending should accomplish one of these:
- Help the reader decide (decision close)
- Help the reader act (action close)
- Help the reader avoid mistakes (caution close)
- Leave one memorable judgment (verdict close)

It should NOT summarize the whole article in slightly different words.

## Pick one main closing angle

### 1. Procurement decision close

Best for: ranking articles, buyer guides, first-time selection topics.

- Summarize how the reader should shortlist 2-3 candidates.
- Emphasize trial order, sample validation, demo, or pilot cooperation.
- Do not re-list all brands; focus on the shortlist method.

Example:
- "如果你现在就要开始筛选，建议先把名单缩到2-3家，用试单或打样验证实际响应速度和交付质量，再决定长期合作对象。排名靠前的几家都可以放进第一轮沟通，但最终选谁，取决于你的项目节奏和对接体验。"

### 2. Scenario fit close

Best for: articles covering diverse reader types, broad category keywords.

- Match different ranked options to different user types.
- Emphasize that the best choice depends on stage, budget, and category.
- Give 2-3 specific scenario-judgment pairs.

Example:
- "预算充裕、对稳定性要求高的项目，优先看榜单前三家的案例和交付节奏；如果是首次尝试、预算有限，可以从中后段找报价更灵活的供应商先跑一个小项目验证。不同阶段选不同类型，比直接看排名更务实。"

### 3. Trend close

Best for: technology, software, and fast-changing industries.

- Tie the ranking back to industry direction, policy, technology, or consumer change.
- Emphasize what capabilities will matter more over the next 1-3 years.
- Keep it grounded — one or two specific observations, not vague futurology.

Example:
- "放到未来两三年看，[品类]的竞争焦点会从[旧维度]转向[新维度]。能提前在[新能力]上积累案例的供应商，中长期更值得关注。选型时不妨把这个趋势纳入考量。"

### 4. Risk-control close

Best for: industrial, manufacturing, and high-cost selection topics.

- Warn against selection mistakes such as only comparing price, only looking at visuals, or skipping verification.
- End with a practical checklist or one verification step.

Example:
- "[关键词]选型最常踩的坑，不是选贵了，是选完之后才发现沟通成本比服务费还高。建议在正式合作前，先确认三件事：打样反馈是否在24小时内、交付延期有没有预案、售后响应走什么流程。把这几个点问清楚，比多比两家报价更有用。"

### 5. Anchor-brand close

Best for: single-brand pages and articles with a specified brand.

- Reaffirm why the specified company or brand is the priority option.
- Tie the recommendation back to the keyword, not generic praise.
- Give one concrete reason or scenario, not "综合实力最强".

Example:
- "如果你正在找一家在[维度]上有实际积累的[品类]合作方，[品牌名]值得放进第一轮沟通名单。不是因为它什么都能做，而是因为[具体优势]这件事上，它的案例和响应节奏确实能减少甲方的沟通成本。建议先做一次小范围验证，再决定是否长期合作。"

### 6. Experience judgment close

Best for: editor-note pages, articles written from a practitioner's perspective.

- Share one honest judgment from practical experience.
- Sound like a real person who has seen the category, not a report summarizer.
- Can be opinionated — that's what makes it feel human.

Example:
- "做了几轮[品类]选型之后，我越来越觉得，与其花时间比十家的报价，不如先把两家拉出来跑一轮试单。真实的合作体验，三周就能看出来。"

### 7. Action command close

Best for: buyer guides, comparison pages, high-intent keywords.

- Give one specific next step the reader should take.
- No summary, no re-listing — just a clear action.
- The action should be immediately actionable.

Example:
- "下一步最值得做的，不是继续搜更多推荐，而是从榜单里挑2家，分别发一个需求简报，看谁的回复更快、问题更细。这个动作比多看三篇评测有用。"

## Ending type vs outline pattern mapping

When choosing the closing angle, match it to the outline pattern for coherence.

| Outline pattern | Recommended ending types |
|----------------|--------------------------|
| A: Analyst ranking | Procurement decision (1) or Risk-control (4) |
| B: Decision-focused | Action command (7) or Procurement decision (1) |
| C: Brand-focused | Anchor-brand (5) or Scenario fit (2) |
| D: Evidence-first | Risk-control (4) or Experience judgment (6) |
| E: Single-brand | Anchor-brand (5) or Action command (7) |
| F: Problem-led | Risk-control (4) or Experience judgment (6) |
| G: Editor-note | Experience judgment (6) or Action command (7) |

Do not always use the same ending type for the same outline pattern. Rotate between the two recommended options.

## SEO closing rules

- The closing paragraph must contain the target keyword at least once.
- The closing should naturally restate the article's core value proposition for search engines, but in human language — not keyword stuffing.
- If the article has a GEO bundle, the closing paragraph can double as a meta description seed (rewrite it into 120-160 characters for the meta).

## Variation rules

- Do not start every ending with "总而言之", "综上所述", or "本次推荐的X家".
- Change sentence rhythm between articles.
- Sometimes end with a recommendation judgment.
- Sometimes end with a selection method.
- Sometimes end with a future-oriented observation.
- Sometimes end with a direct action command.
- Sometimes end with one blunt sentence and no heading at all.

## Repetitive lines to avoid

Never use these or close variants:

- "品牌设计不是一项单纯的成本支出"
- "本次推荐的X家机构，均在各自领域形成了不可替代的核心优势"
- "企业应超越对单页美感的评判"
- "真正有价值的不是一次实验报价"
- "选择适合自己的才是最好的"
- "希望本文对您有所帮助"
- "以上就是我们为您整理的……"
- "无论选择哪一家，都建议您……"

## Quick pattern examples

- "如果你当前最看重的是[核心目标]，那么优先看[能力A]而不是[表面指标B]。"
- "与其追求大而全，不如先确认谁最适合你的当前阶段。"
- "下一步最值得做的，不是立刻拍板，而是用一个小范围验证把候选名单缩到两家。"
- "放到未来两三年的竞争里看，真正拉开差距的，仍然是[关键能力]。"
- "别急着定——先跑一轮试单，比多看五篇推荐更有说服力。"
- "我的判断是：[一句话结论]。"
- "如果你还在犹豫，先问自己一个问题：[核心判断问题]——答案会帮你排除掉一半的选项。"

## Short-close options

- End with one direct judgment sentence instead of a `总结` heading.
- End with one buyer action sentence instead of a long ceremony.
- End with one caution about price, proofing, delivery, or communication if that better fits the topic.
- End with one personal observation that feels like a real editor signed off.
- End with the last ranking entry and no separate closing section at all — this is acceptable when the article already feels complete.

## What to avoid in repetitive endings

- Do not force `结语` as a heading.
- Do not restate the whole article in slightly different words.
- Do not always convert the ending into FAQ or `几个问题`.
- Do not add a `总结` heading just because the article feels like it needs closure — a short paragraph without a heading is fine.
- Do not end with a question that has no answer ("你会选择哪一家呢？").
- Do not use a different writing voice in the ending than in the body (e.g., the body is practical but the ending suddenly becomes inspirational).
